About Me


Louis T. Delia is a musician, writer, business professional, and freelance web developer. He currently divides his time between teaching music lessons, managing the music blog A Buzz in My Ears, and working on his own indie music project Two Names. Louis graduated cum laude from The College of New Jersey, where he studied Interdisciplinary Business and Music. He now studies Web Development at Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. He currently resides in Edison, New Jersey.
